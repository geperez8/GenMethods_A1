About Me

Hello, I am a college student who takes pictures for fun. Most of my images focus on landscapes and environments. My work isn't very abstract; they are simply places that have formed naturally or have been created by humans, which is true for most of my pictures. I am very fond of nature and enjoy seeing it in its rawest form. Most of my images have been taken in San Francisco, Denver, and Chicago. My most recent work has been in San Francisco, which is why the quality is drastically better. Feel free to use any of the pictures; I have no problem with that.
